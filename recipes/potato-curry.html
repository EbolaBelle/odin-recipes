Potato Curry

Ingredients

  • 4 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 2T vegetable oil
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4t curry powder
  • 4t garam masala
  • 2t ground cumin
  • 2t salt
  • 1 (1in) piece fresh ginger root, peeled and minced
  • 1.5t cayenne pepper
  • 1 (15oz) can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 (150z) can peas, drained
  • 1 (14.5oz) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (14oz) can coconut milk
