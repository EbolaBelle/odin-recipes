Candied Nuts

Description

Egg white candied walnuts for Wandi station

Ingredients

  • 2 egg whites
  • 2T extract of choice
  • 5C (440g) walnuts
  • 1C sugar
  • 1/2t salt
  • 3t cinnamon
  • 1/2t cayenne pepper

Process

  1. Preheat oven to 225
  2. Combine dry ingredients
  3. Beat egg whites + extract until fluffy
  4. Toss pecans until fully coates with the beaten whites
  5. Add dry ingredients, toss
  6. Spread on baking sheet and bake for 60mins, turning ever 20mins

Notes

If no walnuts, pecans are acceptable
