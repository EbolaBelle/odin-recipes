Horseradish Aioli

Description

Horseradish aioli for grill station

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 3 yolks
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 75g prepared horseradish
  • 20g fresh lemon juice
  • 15g salt
  • 100g cold water
  • 1000g rice bran oil

Process

  1. Combine eggs, garlic, and horseradish in robot coupe
  2. Slowly drizzle in oil until slowly emulsified
  3. Season with salt and LJ (adjust TT)
  4. Add water as needed
